# GrowthCopilot — LinkedIn Cold Outreach Templates

> Use these for direct outreach to growth leads, PMs, and founders.
> Search for titles like: "Head of Growth," "VP Growth," "Senior PM Growth," "Growth Lead," "Director of Product"
> Best targets: Series A–C companies, product-led growth motion, running paid acquisition across multiple channels.
> Aim for 10–15 outreach messages per week. Expect 2–4 responses. That's normal and enough.

---

## Template A — The morning ritual hook
*Best for: growth leads and senior PMs who would recognize the ritual immediately*

Hi [Name],

Quick question — do you have a morning ritual around your acquisition metrics? Checking a few numbers, deciding whether a drop is real or noise, figuring out if you need to act today?

I built something to replace that ritual and I'm trying to figure out if it's actually useful or just a different way to look at the same data. Would love 10 minutes of honest feedback from someone who'd actually use it.

Demo here if you want a look first: https://growthcopilot.streamlit.app

No pitch, no sales process — just genuinely trying to understand if this matches a real problem.

[Your name]

## Who to target — search terms

**LinkedIn search:**
- "Head of Growth" + Series A/B/C (filter by company size 11–200)
- "VP Product" + "growth" in description
- "Senior PM" + "growth" or "acquisition"
- "Growth Lead" at consumer apps, SaaS, mobile games

**Good company profiles to look for:**
- Consumer social apps (TikTok-heavy acquisition)
- Mobile games (D1 retention obsession)
- SaaS with a freemium/PLG motion
- Marketplace companies (buyer + seller funnel complexity)

**Red flags (skip these):**
- Enterprise companies (6-month sales cycles, not your buyer)
- Companies with 10+ person data teams (they've built this internally)
- Pure B2B outbound sales companies (not the pain point)
